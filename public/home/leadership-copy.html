Founders

Cheruku Srikanth
Founder & CEO

Srikanth is a finance & accounting professional with over 20 years of professional experience encompassing accounting, financial management, audit, internal control implementation, software project management and software product development. He started his career at PwC where he worked in their assurance division & thereafter worked at outsourced arm of an International bank, in Bangalore where he was involved in providing account services to retail customers of the bank in USA.

He then worked in a few small accounting firms for two years before starting his own accounting and bookkeeping services business and diversified the services business to provide legal outsourcing and also software development with focus on developing modules in accounting and financial processes to customers in USA. He has over twenty years of rich & diverse experience in multiple domains encompassing bookkeeping, accounting, financial management, reporting, auditing, outsourcing, software project management and software product development.

Based on his experience in professional services, Srikanth realised that the key challenge faced by businesses, is the non-availability of accurate, reliable and compliant financial data in real-time, which was leading to multiple upstream inefficiencies, delayed reporting and delays in decision making. To solve this problem, Srikanth wanted to find a way to eliminate errors and discrepancies in bookkeeping, accounting & transaction processing and automate the processes in a way that it generates output which is accurate, reliable, compliant and most importantly real-time, which would empower business managers and owners deliver significantly better outcomes for their businesses. This led to him creating IDOS based on over three years of R&D and multiple trial & errors and he developed the functional architecture and the interface whereby every type of transaction is processed by enabling the user to answer simple questions which do not need knowledge of accounting, tax or internal controls and the software completes accounting, ledger posting in compliance to internal controls and tax. He further went on to develop more transformational capabilities which were possible to be built on core of IDOS including eco-system engagement and liquidity optimisation which enable transformational changes, in terms of how accounting and financial management is handled by businesses of all sizes and is also transforming multiple elements of commerce, accounting and financial operations including digital lending, bill discounting, borrower management by banks, vendor and procurement management, dealer & distributor management, customer engagement, hub & spoke accounting, real-time consolidation of accounts, remote and real-time audit, automated internal audit and so on.

Srikanth is the product owner at IDOS and his focus is on product development, strategic alliances and business expansion. He is a frequent speaker at prestigious forums on entrepreneurship, fintech and digital transformation in accounting & financial management and is a Member of National Committee on IT & ITES at CII.

Alexander Reife
Co-founder & COO

Alex is the co-founder of IDOS and he conceived the idea and developed the functional architecture to embed internal financial controls into accounting process to support real-time internal control compliance and also developed the functional architecture to embed expenditure budgets during transaction processing. He comes with several years’ experience in project management, budgeting, financial analysis, audit and international development.

He holds a Bachelors Degree in Psychology & Pre-Med from Georgetown University, Double Master’s in Public Policy & Administration and International Relations from The George Washington University, USA and Maters in Marketing from St.Petersburg State University, Russia and a PhD in Economics from Moscow, Russia.

He started his career at World Bank and during his corporate career spanning over 20 years, he also worked with contractors involved in International Development Projects and managed bookkeeping, financial management, payroll, internal audit and reporting for multiple projects spread across South America, Eastern Europe and South East Asia and gained significant experience in matters related to accounting and financial management, remote project management and issues related to data management and reporting. Having worked across multiple geographies he understands the cultural nuances that exist in work environments in different countries, unique accounting & reporting challenges at Head Quarters and at branch or project offices, periodic reporting and compliance challenges where cross-cultural and cross-country professionals are involved and he brings all this learning and experience into IDOS to ensure that the product can meet the requirements of such business operations and can become a tool which can make accounting & financial management processes frictionless for businesses with multiple branches and projects. As co-founder of IDOS, he manages product strategy for International markets, strategic collaborations and Business development and is also a guest faculty for Economics at a University in St.Petersburg, Russia where he occasionally delivers lectures.
