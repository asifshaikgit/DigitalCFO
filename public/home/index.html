The real pain points for CFOs

Bad data is a universal problem for all business entities, irrespective of size and sector.

Be it outdated, inaccurate, incomplete, or inconsistent data - poor quality data adversely impacts organisational performance.

Bad data causes skewed analysis, suboptimal decision-making, cost inefficiencies, missed business opportunities, revenue loss and trust deficiency amongst customers.

Risk of inadequate security & lack of data privacy

Limited availability of integrated, scalable, 100% automated financial software

Challenges in managing the changing expectations of multiple stakeholders

Lack of single-point access to critical key performance indicators to aid decision making

Complex solutions that often lack cross-functional domain expertise

High costs associated with enterprise-level software implementation

Bottlenecks in the adoption of best practices in financial management

Absence of role-based access aggravating fraud risk and duplication errors

IDOS solves the root problem and eliminates bad data at the fundamental bookkeeping and transaction processing stage.
