Microsoft Excel Integration

Integration with Microsoft Excel and Mobile applications

Overview: Microsoft Office is a popular software preferred by customers, retail and institutional alike. Microsoft Excel, with its advanced features in data analysis, reports generation with table, graphs and other visualisations is a convenient tool.

Challenges: With digitally mobile taking centerstage, business applications are expected to be mobile friendly with all software capabilities extensible to mobile devices as well. IDOS was required to provide integrations whereby accounting and financial data could be easily analysed on Microsoft Excel and specific actions could be handled through mobile applications.

IDOS Solution: IDOS developed integration with Excel on-line whereby IDOS users could conveniently login to IDOS directly from Microsoft Excel on-line extension and view reports with corresponding graphs, with just a single click.

Results: IDOS developed a one-of-its-kind capability to integrate with the Microsoft Kaizala app with provision for GST related action to be completed via ones mobile.
