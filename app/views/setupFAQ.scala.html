|--  How to add Discount for a Product / Customer?

@(configParams.getCompanyApp()) allows you set up discount with reference to either item or customer.

Discount allowed for a Product:

  1. Under 'Options' - click on 'Chart of Accounts'
  2. Click on heading 'Income' and again click on + sign. Select the product for which you want to allow discount
  3. Select the product from the dropdown which is under the heading 'Branch / Max Discount (%) for walk-in Customer'
  4. Click on Add / update of Chart of Accounts

When you allow discount for a Product, you can even allow discounts for Walk-in Customers.

Discount allowed for Customer:

  1. Under 'Options' click on 'Customer'
  2. Select a Customer and now click on 'Edit'
  3. Under the heading 'Item of Sale / Discount%' (6th column), click on the dropdown and enter the discount percentage
  4. Click on 'Add / update Customer'

This is the maximum discount allowed for the product. You can override the discount while creating transactions.
