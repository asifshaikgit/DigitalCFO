Please use login credentials sent through e-mail after the account set up.

Step 1: Under 'Options' click on 'Transactions'.


Step 2: Click on tab 'Create Transaction'

Select 'Sell on cash and payment now' option from Dropdown.


Step 3: Select Branch, Project (if you want to allocate), Type of supply, Customer, Place of Supply and

PO reference if any and Product (Income item) from the dropdown below and barcode will

auto populate if it has been given at the time of creation of the item.


Step 4: Price will be auto populated (based on Income setup) and then enter the no. of units.

GST will be computed automatically.

Check Net amount, Receipt details and add remarks if any.


Step 5: Scan and 'Upload supporting document if any.


Step 6: Click on 'Submit for Accounting' and Journal entries are posted automatically.

Discount allowed for a Customer:

Step 1: Under 'Options' click on 'Customer'.


Step 2: Select a Customer and now click on 'Edit'.


Step 3: Under the heading 'Item of Sale / Discount%' (6th column), click on the dropdown and enter the discount percentage


Step 4: Click on 'Add / update Customer'.


  This is the maximum discount allowed for the product. You cannot override the discount while creating transactions.
